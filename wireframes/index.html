Hostel Management — Wireframes

Each role sees only their own screens in the real app. Below is grouped by role for review.

👑
Management
3 users · Read-everything, write-only-config · Bottom nav: Today · Trends · Wardens · Settings

Doesn't touch the daily flow. Owns the dashboard, configures the schedule and approvers, reviews exceptions, and approves special-day outings only when escalated.

📊 Today

Live counters + red alerts and exceptions only. The main screen.

📈 Trends (planned)

Weekly/monthly — warden reliability, wastage patterns, attendance averages, vendor scorecard.

👥 Wardens (planned)

Per-warden scorecards + drill into a warden's full day with call/message buttons.

📜 Activity log (planned)

Full audit timeline of everything today — done + missed. Filters by warden / type.

⚙️ Settings (planned)

Students · Users · Approvers · Schedule · Pump sessions · Holiday calendar.

🧑‍🏫
Warden
3–4 users · Do-the-work · Bottom nav: Tasks · Attendance · Sick · Laundry · Me

Hands-on operators. Complete time-locked tasks, take 5 attendances, manage sick bay, distribute laundry, handle kitchen/supply/pump. Camera-only uploads, locked outside their window.

📋 Today's tasks

Time-ordered checklist with status badges. Tap any open task to act.

📸 Task action — Yoga photo

Camera-only upload screen. Window countdown. No gallery access.

🔢 Attendance entry

Count-based. Auto-tally and discrepancy check. Cross-warden verification at lunch.

🤒 Sick bay

Add sick student. Track parent call. Set outcome (Resting / Sent home / Doctor / Recovered).

🧺 Laundry distribution

Per-student give/hold during 9:00–9:30 pm window. Unclaimed flow for absent students.

🚪 Start outing (3 steps)

Parent at gate flow: pick student → parent OTP → reason + send to staff approvers.

🚰 Pump session (planned)

Live ON→OFF session card. Photo at each stage. Duration auto-tracked.

✅
Staff approver
2–3 users · Gatekeeper · Bottom nav: Approvals · History · Me

Single focused job: approve outings. Any one of them can release a student. For special-day outings, must pick a reason chip first. Read-only view of sick context when relevant.

✅ Approvals inbox

Pending outings with parent OTP state. Reason chips. Approve / reject.

📜 History (planned)

All outings today/week/month. Read-only. Search by student.

👨‍👩‍👧
Parent
Per student · No app login

No app, no screens. Receives WhatsApp/SMS for sick reports and outing approvals. Verified via OTP at the gate for pickup.

🎓
Student
150 users · No app in Phase 1

Phase 1: stored as a registry record only. Phase 4 (optional): read-only view of own laundry status and attendance — deferred until core flow is proven.
